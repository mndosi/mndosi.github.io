Mankwe Ndosi works in sound and soil as a Culture Worker and Musician. She is focused on forgotten and marginalized plants, people, and ways of knowing.

Mankwe uses creative and embodied practices to reconnect and sturdy our relationships with our purpose, our people, our legacies, and our earth.
Mankwe Ndosi is an Evokalist / Composer / Culture Worker of sound and soil.
